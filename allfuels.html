For the "Efficiency" section of our project, we have only taken three examples, namely: Gasoline, Diesel, Hydrogen, and Electricity. Moreover, we also only took two distinct variables to compare the energy efficiency of these fuels:

  • Gasoline Gallon Equivalent (GGE)
  • Energy Content
  • Pump Octane Number
This page contains data for various other fuels and their data with respect to the variables above. Definitions of all the terms have been provided below in this page. All Data has been obtained from The Alternative Fuels Data Center, maintained by The U.S. Department of Energy.
